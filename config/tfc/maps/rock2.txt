The Rock - Capture The Flag Variant

Objective: Steal the Keycard from the enemy
Warden's office. Take it across to the other
side of the enemy Base and hit the switch to
release the Gas. Don't bring the Keycard back
to your own Base.

Scoring: 15 points for each Gas Release.

Other Notes: To survive the Gas, get a
protective suit from behind the marked 
doors, or hide under the water.
