Hunted - Assassination / Escort

Objective: The Hunted must reach the truck
alive. The Assassins need to kill The Hunted.
The Bodyguards need to protect The Hunted.

Scoring: If The Hunted dies, the Assassins get
25 points. If The Hunted reaches the truck, 
he and the Bodyguards get 50 points.

Other Notes: Whenever The Hunted dies or
makes it to the truck, all players are moved
back to their starting positions.
